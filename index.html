Welcome to AIC Kapkai Secondary School

Located in the heart of Kwanza, Trans Nzoia County, our school is committed to academic excellence and spiritual growth under Christian values. We empower students to become responsible citizens of tomorrow.

About Us

Mission: To provide holistic, value-based education to nurture responsible and God-fearing citizens.

Vision: To be a center of academic excellence guided by Christian values.

AIC Kapkai Secondary School has been serving the community with dedication, aiming to mold students both academically and morally. We take pride in our disciplined environment and hardworking staff.
